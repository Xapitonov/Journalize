<h2>Features</h2>

<ul>
  <li>Renders in Markdown (images included)</li>
  <li>Automatic pseudo-Markdown rendering</li>
  <li>Automatically generates the next starting character in lists</li>
  <li>Import, export, and share</li>
  <li>Supports geo-stamping of entries</li>
  <li>Custom tags for the entries</li>
  <li>Browse entries</li>
  <li>Search entries based on content and tags</li>
  <li>Quickly open previous entry</li>
  <li>Automatically open new entry after reopening app after custom duration</li>
  <li>Black and White mode, both in greyscale</li>
</ul>
